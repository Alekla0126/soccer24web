How to Delete Your Account

To delete your account, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Account Settings page in the app or website.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page.
  3. Click on Delete Your Account.
  4. Confirm your decision in the confirmation dialog.

Note: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. If you have any questions, please contact our support team.
